foodXchange is the community-owned app built by The Food Investors Society to help people cut through
today's profit-driven food landscape and make sense of what's on offer in our shops, kitchens, and neighbourhoods.

We don't tell people what to eat. That's not the problem. The problem is a food system stacked with confusing labels,
engineered convenience, and marketing that pushes cheap, unhealthy processed options.

foodXchange gives you the tools to rebalance the system in your favour.

Let's be honest, making good food choices is easier said than done.

Explore the App's Features

FoodX

Scan and discover the
best-value, least processed foods

  •  Find the best-priced minimally processed foods
  •  Use small swaps to steer your shop toward
         less processed choices
  •  Filter results by your food preferences
  •  Get notified when favourites go on offer
  •  View detailed product info with one tap
  •  Quickly add items to your shopping list or meal plan

My List

Turn every shopping list into lasting
value for your health and wallet

  •  Create and manage shopping lists with ease
  •  Automatically grouped by cheapest retailer
  •  Sort by store or product category
  •  Check off items as you shop
  •  Share lists and sync offline

Meal Plans

Build your health portfolio through
simple, homemade cooking

  •  Adopt a preset meal plan or create your own
  •  Plan meals weeks ahead with ease
  •  Convert plans into shopping lists instantly
  •  Share meal plans effortlessly
  •  Access offline and sync when back online

Recipes & Cooking Hacks

Create better meals and lasting
health from everyday ingredients

  •  Discover quick 'make your own' alternatives to
         common ultra-processed foods
  •  Filter recipes by your available ingredients
  •  View details and add recipes directly to meal plans
  •  Watch cook-along sessions
  •  Import your own recipes automatically

My Spaces & Items

Track, update and organise your pantry
to protect food investments

  •  Create and manage pantry spaces
  •  View all items within each space
  •  Filter items by expiry status
  •  Add, edit, or update pantry items easily
  •  Auto-update pantry when items are checked off

Waste

Track expiry and waste to make
groceries last longer

  •  View all expiring items in one place
  •  Track weekly and yearly food wastage
         visual charts
  •  See the total monetary value of wasted items
  •  Identify the top contributors to waste

My Neighbourhood

Connect with your local food neighbourhood

  •  Locate local community food activities and
         specialist independent food outlets
  •  See upcoming food events on the map
  •  Link to connect with food activities, specialist
         outlets and events in Groups

My Groups

Build a community that grows through
shared ideas and healthier living

  •  Connect with and support a like-minded
         growing community
  •  Browse your groups, trending groups
         and featured groups
  •  View group details and join instantly
  •  Create new topics and share your ideas
