Join our growing network of organisations united by a shared commitment to a healthier,

Benefits:

✓ Your organisation's logo displayed on our website as

    mission and values

Supporter Application Form

Supporters are organisations that champion our mission for a fairer, more sustainable food future. Your support helps strengthen our movement and signals your commitment to reshaping the UK's food environment. Please note, Supporters are not Society members and do not hold shares or voting rights.

6. Supporter Commitment (required)

“As a Supporter of The Food Investors Society Ltd, our organisation shares the ambition of building a better food future. We agree to uphold the values of fairness, transparency, and community benefit. We understand that Supporter status does not confer Society membership, voting rights, or shares.”



7. Data Protection and Communications (required)

We will process your organisation's contact details and the personal data of nominated representatives for the purposes of administering your supporter relationship with The Food Investors Society, coordinating collaborative activity, and maintaining appropriate records. This processing is carried out in accordance with UK GDPR and is necessary for us to manage our working relationship and meet our legitimate organisational obligations.


Marketing and Optional Updates (optional)

Consent to receive marketing communications may be withdrawn at any time without affecting your organisation's supporter relationship.


8. Authorised Signatory Declaration (required)

Must be completed by the person permitted to make public commitments on behalf of the organisation.
