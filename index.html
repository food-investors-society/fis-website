The Challenge We're Tackling

Across the UK, people are surrounded by food that's quick, cheap, and heavily promoted, but often not great for our health.

Research makes it clear: the more heavily marketed industrial foods in our diets, the higher our risk of poor health, including obesity, heart disease, Type 2 diabetes and poor mental wellbeing.

It's not about willpower. It's about what's available, convenient, and marketed most loudly. We're here to make good food the easier choice.
