foodX Community brings together two types of Community Food Partners:

Community Food Partner

(Non-Profit)

Organisations working to improve access to good food, reduce food insecurity, or build local skills, such as:

  • food hubs
  • community kitchens
  • cooking skills projects
  • growing initiatives

They're listed for free because they reinvest their work back into the community.

Sign-Up

Community Food Partner

(For-Profit)

Independently run outlets aligned with our mission, including:

  • specialist grocers
  • refill shops
  • independent bakers & butchers
  • farmers' markets
  • cultural food retailers

They help diversify the high street and keep good food options alive in your neighbourhood.

Why Local Food Matters

Local food connections bring value to all of us. They:

  • strengthen neighbourhood resilience
  • support small businesses
  • preserve food traditions
  • provide more nutritious meal options
  • keep money circulating locally
  • create social connection and belonging

When we invest in local food activity, we're investing in healthier communities.
