Building a Better Food Future.
One We All Own Together.

The Food Investors Society is a Community Benefit Society (CBS); a non-profit, community-owned organisation where every member gets an equal voice. And we built foodXchange, our digital platform, to give people the tools to navigate the modern food environment with clarity, confidence, and value.

Why a Co-operative?

Our food system is heavily shaped by commercial interests. We believe the alternative must be owned
by the people it serves. That's why we chose a co-operative legal structure.

Mission

To empower people and communities to make informed, affordable, and enjoyable food choices.

Vision

A future where:

  • healthier food is easier to access than heavily processed alternatives
  • local food culture thrives
  • people feel more connected to what they eat
  • communities share in the benefits of better food
  • the food system supports long-term wellbeing, fairness, and sustainability

We call this CommonwHealth. Shared wealth, created through good food.

Our Story

The co-founders came together with the belief that the UK needs a fairer, more transparent food system. What began as a conversation about ultra-processed foods and public health grew into a mission to create a platform that supports informed choice, community power, and system change.

Since then, our journey has included:

  • commissioning a full technical discovery
  • partnering with UK universities to build a proof-of-concept website and app
  • collaborating with community food networks
  • engaging public health experts, developers, and behaviour scientists
  • preparing for a limited market pilot and community share offer

This is just the beginning, and we want people like you to help shape what comes next.
