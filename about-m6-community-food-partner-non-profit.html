5. Conflict of Interest & Affiliations (required)

To ensure transparency and independence, we ask all partners to declare any relationships or activities that could be perceived as a conflict of interest with the Society's mission.

Do you have any affiliations that may represent a potential conflict of interest?

Declaration:

6. Partnership Commitment (required)

“As a Community Food Partner, our organisation shares the values and mission of The Food Investors Society Ltd and supports efforts to build a healthier, fairer, and more sustainable food system.”



7. Data Protection and Communications (required)

We will process your organisation's details, including the contact information of your nominated representative, for the purposes of administering your listing on the My Neighbourhood directory, maintaining accurate public information, coordinating relevant partnership activity, and keeping appropriate records. This processing is carried out in accordance with UK GDPR and is necessary for us to manage the directory and our working relationship with you.


Marketing and Optional Updates (optional)

You may withdraw consent to receive marketing communications at any time without affecting your directory listing or partnership status.



8. Authorised Signatory Declaration (required)

This section must be completed by a person authorised to enter into agreements on behalf of the organisation (may be the same as the nominated representative).
