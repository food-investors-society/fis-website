foodXchange is built and owned by its members through The Food
Investors Society, a non-profit UK Community Benefit Society.
That means:

  • No profit-driven shareholders
  • One member, one vote
  • Full accountability and transparency
  • A mission-first approach focused on health, fairness, and community benefit

By joining, you're not just signing up.
You're becoming a co-owner in a shared mission for change.
